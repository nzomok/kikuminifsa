Follow these simple steps to use our PayBill number

  1. Open sim toolkit and select M-PESA

  2. Select Lipa na M-PESA

  3. Go to Pay Bill

  4. Select Enter Business Number

  5. Enter our Business number 713243

  6. Click Account Number

  7. Enter your ID NUMBER as your account number eg 37258120

  8. ClickOK,and in the next step enter amount

  9. Enter your M-PESA PIN to confirm the payment
